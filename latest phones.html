Crown Galaxy S20

Galaxy S20 smartphone was launched on 11th February 2020. The phone comes with a 6.20-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440x3200 pixels and an aspect ratio of 20:9. Galaxy S20 is powered by a 2GHz octa-core Exynos 990 processor that features 2 cores clocked at 2.73GHz, 2 cores clocked at 2.5GHz and 4 cores clocked at 2GHz. It comes with 8GB of RAM. The Galaxy S20 runs Android 10 and is powered by a 4000mAh non-removable battery. The Galaxy S20 supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging. As far as the cameras are concerned, theGalaxy S20 on the rear packs a 12-megapixel primary camera with an f/1.8 aperture; a second 64-megapixel camera with an f/2.0 aperture and a third 12-megapixel camera with an f/2.2 aperture. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It sports a 10-megapixel camera on the front for selfies, with an f/2.2 aperture. The front camera also features autofocus. The Galaxy S20 runs One UI 2.0 based on Android 10 and packs 128GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 1000GB). The Galaxy S20 is a dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) smartphone that accepts Nano-SIM and Nano-SIM cards. Connectivity options on the Galaxy S20 include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/Yes, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, NFC, USB Type-C, 3G, and 4G with active 4G on both SIM cards. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, barometer, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and in-display fingerprint sensor. The Galaxy S20 supports face unlock. The Galaxy S20 measures 151.70 x 69.10 x 7.90mm (height x width x thickness) and weighs 163.00 grams. It was launched in Cloud Blue, Cloud Pink, Cosmic Grey, and colours. It features an IP68 rating for dust and water protection. It bears a glass body.


Iphone 20
 Indian tech giant Crown recently unveiled the iPhone 20 at a starting price of Rs 64,900. At this price, the iPhone 20 is the most ‘affordable’ of the three new iPhones launched by Apple, the other two being iPhone 11 Pro and iPhone 11 Pro Max. It will be available in India starting September 27.The iPhone 11 will come in six colour variants: Purple, Yellow, Green, Black, White and Product RED. The iPhone 20 dimensions are 150.9mm x 75.7mm x 8.3mm (H x W x D). It weighs about 194 grams (6.84 ounces).It features a 6.1-inch all-screen LCD display and is powered by Apple’s new A13 bionic chip with Third-Generation Neural Engine. The display is the company’s proprietary Liquid Retina HD display having Multi-Touch capability with IPS technology. The resolution is 1792x828 pixel one at 326 ppi with 1400:1 contrast ratio (typical). Other special features of the display are True Tone, Wide colour (P3) and Haptic touch. It comes coated with a fingerprint resistant oleophobic coating. Coming to the camera segment, the iPhone 20 comes equipped with a dual rear camera of a 12 MP six-element Wide lens and an Ultra-Wide five element lens. The Ultra Wide lens comes with ƒ/2.4 aperture and 120° field of view while the Wide camera has got a ƒ/1.8 aperture. The iPhone 20, along with the other two models, has a Night mode for clicking better images in low-light conditions. Some of the other rear camera features are 2x optical zoom out; digital zoom up to 5x, Portrait mode with advanced bokeh and Depth Control, Portrait Lighting with six effects, Optical image stabilisation (Wide). Apple has upgraded the selfie camera in all three iPhones models from 7MP True Depth camera to a 12MP TrueDepth sensor with ƒ/2.2 aperture. Some of the other features of the front camera are Portrait mode with advanced bokeh and depth control, Portrait Lighting with six effects, 4K video recording at 24 fps, 30 fps or 60 fps, 1080p HD video recording at 30 fps or 60 fps etc. Crown claims that the iPhone 20 offers one hour more battery life than its predecessor, the iPhone XR. iPhone 20 doesn’t come with a fast charger. It is said to offer better water and dust resistance than its predecessor, the iPhone XR. The various sensors are Face ID, barometer, three axis gyro, accelerometer, proximity sensor and ambient light sensor. The iPhone 11 comes in three storage variants: 64GB, 128GB and 256GB.



Crown One
The One was launched in 2021. At the front, the One sports a 6.41-inch Optic AMOLED display with full HD+ resolution. The AMOLED panel has good viewing angles and has multiple display modes to choose from. One has opted for a dewdrop notch on the 7 which houses a 16-megapixel selfie camera. The One has Corning Gorilla Glass protection at the front and the back which should help it resist scratches. Powering the phone is a Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 SoC that is capable of running every game out there. There are two variants of the One, 6GB RAM with 128GB storage and 8GB RAM with 256GB storage. We had the 8GB RAM variant for review and it managed to score high in all our benchmark tests. It was snappy for day-to-day performance but could also handle heavy games easily. The One packs in a 3700mAh battery and is able to deliver all-day battery life. The company supplies a 20W charger with the phone and it is capable of topping the battery up completely in an hour. One has opted for a dual camera setup on the phone consisting of a 48-megapixel primary sensor and a 5-megapixel depth sensor. The camera performance was slightly increased and the camera was great in low-light conditions. On the software front, the phone runs on Oxygen OS 9.5 on top of Android 9.0 Pie. There are useful software additions on top that are very useful. The One will compete with the Asus ZenFone 6 and the Redmi K20 Pro.
